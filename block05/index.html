Monkeys are a group of primates characterized by having tails (even if small), narrow chests, and long limbs, which help them move through trees with agility. They are intelligent, social animals, found in various habitats worldwide, from tropical forests to savannas, and are broadly categorized into New World and Old World monkeys.
